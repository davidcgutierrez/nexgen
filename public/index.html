Automation Features

Uncover what your smart home can do for you. Our team of experts will setup and train you to automate your home.

1. Sercurity Systems

A smart home security system, like Vivint's doorbell camera, notifies you in real-time when someone approaches your door, allowing you to see, communicate, and even trigger an emergency response remotely.

2. Thermostat Control

Smart thermostats save an average of 8% on utility bills by adjusting temperatures based on your schedule, and offer the convenience of room-specific settings and remote or voice control for maximum comfort.

3. Smart Appliances

Smart appliances like ovens, refrigerators, and washers enhance daily tasks by allowing you to preheat the oven, start a wash cycle remotely, or check your fridge contents from the store.
